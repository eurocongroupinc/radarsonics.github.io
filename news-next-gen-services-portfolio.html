Radarsonics strengthens next-generation network services portfolio.

Illustrative press-style announcement

Radarsonics announced an expanded services portfolio bringing together network modernisation, cloud, security and operations under integrated programmes.

The approach is designed for operators and enterprises that want a single partner to support them from planning and design through rollout and long-term managed services.

Integrated network, cloud and security capabilities

Customers increasingly expect infrastructure, platforms and applications to be treated as a connected system rather than isolated projects. Radarsonics has invested in cross-domain teams and shared delivery frameworks to reflect this reality.

Ongoing focus on reliability

Building on nearly three decades of telecom experience, the updated portfolio keeps reliability and operational discipline at the core while opening up room for new automation and tooling.
