CAREERS

Build networks, platforms and operations that matter.

At Radarsonics, engineers, architects, project managers and operations specialists work together on programmes that keep people connected and businesses running.

Why work at Radarsonics?

  • Telecom-grade problems with real impact on millions of users.
  • Cross-functional teams spanning networks, cloud, security and software.
  • Structured learning paths, certifications and mentoring.
  • Opportunities to work on long-running programmes and greenfield builds.

Life at Radarsonics

Teams operate from Mumbai, Bengaluru, Kolkata, Noida, Chennai and Ahmedabad, often collaborating in distributed squads aligned to customers and programmes.

We combine the discipline required for 24x7 operations with the curiosity needed to adopt new tools and approaches.
