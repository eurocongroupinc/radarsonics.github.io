CSR & SUSTAINABILITY

Connecting people responsibly, for the long term.

As a telecom and IT services company, Radarsonics touches millions of lives indirectly every day. Our responsibility is not just to build and run networks, but to help ensure they are inclusive, efficient and environmentally conscious.

Digital inclusion

We work with operators, partners and local communities to extend connectivity to under-served regions and groups.

  • Support for rural and semi-urban network rollouts
  • Connectivity for schools, health centres and community spaces
  • Employee volunteering for digital literacy programmes

Environment & energy

Telecom and IT infrastructure consume power. We focus on optimising energy use and encouraging sustainable practices.

  • Energy-efficient designs for sites and data rooms
  • Support for renewable and hybrid energy models
  • Monitoring to track and reduce carbon impact over time

People & communities

Our teams live and work in the communities we serve. We invest in their safety, growth and ability to give back.

  • Structured health & safety programmes for field teams
  • Learning paths and certifications for engineers and operators
  • Local initiatives driven by office-level CSR committees

OUR CSR JOURNEY

From informal initiatives to structured programmes.

What began as ad-hoc contributions by teams on the ground is gradually evolving into focused, measurable programmes across our hubs.

Early years

Grassroots support from project teams

Field teams and project offices contributed to local schools, health camps and community events around major rollouts.

Formalisation

Dedicated CSR focus at leadership level

Senior leaders began setting aside time, budgets and governance for structured CSR efforts tied to long-term themes.

Today

Company-wide programmes with local ownership

Each office runs initiatives under shared themes — digital inclusion, education and sustainability — with central support for measurement and best-practice sharing.
