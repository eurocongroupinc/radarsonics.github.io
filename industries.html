INDUSTRIES

Where we apply our telecom & IT experience.

We work with organisations that depend on connectivity and digital infrastructure for their mission — from nation-scale operators to specialised enterprises.

Telecom operators

Supporting mobile and fixed-line operators with network rollout, optimisation, managed services and IT transformation.

  • RAN & transport network programmes
  • OSS/BSS integration & automation
  • NOC and field-force operations

Digital & technology providers

Helping cloud, content and technology providers build resilient connectivity and platform foundations.

  • Multi-cloud & edge connectivity
  • Data centre & interconnect solutions
  • Monitoring, SRE & DevOps support

Banking & financial services

Ensuring that branches, ATMs, payment systems and employees stay connected, secure and compliant.

  • Secure branch & ATM connectivity
  • Contact centre & customer experience
  • Compliance-ready infrastructure operations

Government & public sector

Supporting smart city, e-governance, citizen service and critical infrastructure programmes.

  • City-wide and campus networks
  • Data centre and cloud transformation
  • Integrated command & control centres

Manufacturing & industrial

Networks, industrial IoT and applications that connect plants, warehouses and logistics networks.

  • Factory and warehouse connectivity
  • Industrial IoT & monitoring
  • Integration with MES/ERP systems

Healthcare & education

Enabling connected care and modern learning experiences, backed by secure, reliable infrastructure.

  • Campus and multi-site connectivity
  • Collaboration and telepresence solutions
  • Secure access to critical applications
