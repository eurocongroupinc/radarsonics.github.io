GLOBAL PRESENCE

Rooted in India, supporting programmes across regions.

Radarsonics is headquartered in Mumbai with delivery hubs in Bengaluru, Kolkata, Noida, Chennai and Ahmedabad. From these centres, our teams support networks, platforms and transformation programmes in India and beyond.

Delivery hubs and focus areas

Each Radarsonics hub specialises in a mix of engineering, operations and programme leadership capabilities. Together, they form a unified delivery spine that can support long-running, complex programmes.

  • Mumbai (Headquarters): Leadership, strategy, governance and key customer offices.
  • Bengaluru: Network engineering, cloud, DevOps and platform teams.
  • Kolkata: Eastern region field operations, NOC and support.
  • Noida: Northern region rollouts, enterprise connectivity and programme offices.
  • Chennai: Data centre, core network and managed services hub.
  • Ahmedabad: Western region projects and customer engagement.
