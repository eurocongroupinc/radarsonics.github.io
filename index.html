OUR APPROACH

From network and IT inventory to measurable outcomes.

Every Radarsonics engagement follows a structured but flexible sequence. We bring tools and templates, but we tailor them to your context.

01

Discover & baseline

Map your networks, infrastructure, applications and dependencies into an understandable inventory and health view.

  • Architecture and configuration discovery
  • Operational process and tool assessment
  • Risk, cost and opportunity analysis
02

Co-design the target state

Define what “good” looks like for coverage, performance, security and experience over the next 3–5 years.

  • Network and IT reference architectures

03

Execute with shared ownership

Roll out change in a way that protects day-to-day operations while still making tangible progress every quarter.

  • Structured testing and change windows
  • Regular steering forums and reporting
04

Operate & evolve

  • Integrated NOC / SOC / cloud operations
  • Continuous optimisation and automation
  • Quarterly value and improvement reviews

QUESTIONS

Frequently asked about working with Radarsonics.

Do you work only with telecom operators?

No. While our heritage is deeply telecom, we increasingly work with enterprises, financial institutions, government programmes and digital-native providers that need telecom-grade reliability.

Can you take over operations from an existing vendor?

Yes. Many of our engagements involve transitioning services from another provider. We follow a structured discovery, shadow and transition approach before assuming full responsibility.

How do you work with internal teams?

We prefer joint teams. Internal experts keep ownership of knowledge and strategy, while Radarsonics brings additional capacity, specialised skills and battle-tested practices.

Can you support programmes outside India?

Yes. Using our Indian hubs and selected local partners, we support rollouts and operations in multiple regions while maintaining a common delivery spine.

Planning your next phase of network or IT transformation?

Whether you're expanding coverage, consolidating data centres or rebuilding customer channels, Radarsonics can help you plan, build and run the next generation of your digital infrastructure.
