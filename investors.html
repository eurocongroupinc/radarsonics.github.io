INVESTOR INFORMATION

Building a resilient, long-term services business.

Radarsonics operates as a privately held company with a long-term orientation, reinvesting in people, platforms and partnerships that strengthen our telecom and IT capabilities.

Business model

Our revenues are primarily derived from long-term service and managed services contracts, complemented by project-based transformation work.

  • Multi-year managed services engagements
  • Project-based rollout and transformation programmes
  • Solution and platform licensing where applicable

Governance

Governance at Radarsonics emphasises ethics, transparency and responsible delivery in safety- and mission-critical environments.

  • Independent board oversight on strategy and risk
  • Formal programme and account governance structures
  • Focus on compliance, security and sustainability
