PLATFORMS

Reusable building blocks for networks, operations and cloud.

Beyond bespoke projects, Radarsonics invests in repeatable platforms and accelerators that help customers move faster while maintaining reliability and control.

RadarConnect™

Network & service assurance fabric.

RadarConnect aggregates telemetry from networks, infrastructure and applications into a unified view designed for operations teams.

  • Multi-vendor, multi-domain data collection
  • Topology and dependency mapping
  • Health, incident and experience dashboards

RadarEdge™

Edge & site automation toolkit.

For towers, POPs, branches and micro data centres, RadarEdge brings standardised, automated workflows for deployment and lifecycle operations.

  • Golden templates for site designs and configurations
  • Automated provisioning, testing and sign-off
  • Remote monitoring, access and control

RadarCloud™

Cloud landing zone & DevOps starter kit.

RadarCloud accelerates the creation of secure, governed environments for applications across public cloud and data centres.

  • Landing zone blueprints for multiple providers
  • Integrated CI/CD, security and observability patterns
  • Guardrails that balance developer freedom with control

HOW WE USE PLATFORMS

Not products in a box, but accelerators in programmes.

Radarsonics platforms are designed to plug into real-world environments alongside your existing tools, vendors and processes.

Designed for coexistence

We expect complex landscapes. Platforms are built to work with multiple vendors and toolchains rather than replace everything at once.

Implemented in phases

Capabilities are rolled out alongside programmes, so teams can adopt them gradually, see value and adjust.

Supported by our teams

Radarsonics engineers implement, operate and evolve the platforms, often in a managed-services model with clear SLAs.
