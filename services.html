SERVICES

End-to-end services across telecom and IT.

Radarsonics offers a complete spectrum of services — from greenfield rollout to 24/7 operations — designed to work across multi-vendor, multi-technology environments.

Network planning & optimisation

Planning and optimisation services that ensure capacity, coverage and quality are aligned to how customers actually use the network.

  • RAN planning & optimisation for 4G/5G
  • Drive tests, benchmarking & reporting
  • Capacity planning and spectrum efficiency

Rollout & field services

Field-ready teams and playbooks to build, integrate and maintain telecom and IT infrastructure at scale.

  • Site acquisition, design & construction
  • Node, tower and small-cell deployments
  • Preventive and corrective maintenance

Managed network services

Shared or dedicated NOC models that manage networks and platforms, leaving your teams free to focus on innovation.

  • 24/7 monitoring & incident management
  • Performance & capacity management
  • Service assurance & SLA reporting

Cloud & infrastructure services

Design, migration and steady-state operations for hybrid data centre and cloud environments.

  • Cloud strategy & migration
  • Data centre consolidation & modernisation
  • Hybrid operations, backup & DR

Enterprise ICT solutions

Solutions for enterprises to connect, collaborate and secure their employees and customers.

  • LAN/WAN & SD-WAN solutions
  • Unified communication & collaboration
  • Contact centre & CX platforms

Cybersecurity & SOC

Security services built for the realities of telecom and enterprise networks, with shared or dedicated SOC options.

  • Security architecture & hardening
  • SIEM, SOAR & threat monitoring
  • Vulnerability management & response

Application & platform engineering

Software development teams that understand network and IT constraints, building scalable, API-first platforms.

  • Customer & partner portals
  • OSS/BSS extensions & integrations
  • Data analytics & reporting solutions

Consulting & advisory

Cross-functional teams that help you translate business objectives into pragmatic transformation roadmaps.

  • Network & IT estate assessments
  • Business case & roadmap creation
  • Operating model & process redesign
